Make your own floating head.

The same treatment as the head on my homepage: an oval cutout, the ink-grey look, and a pair of living eyes that follow your cursor. Four small steps. Everything happens right here in your browser — your photo never leaves this page.

Step 1 of 4 · The photo

Start with your face.

A straight-on photo works best: face the camera, even light, eyes open. Selfies are perfect.

Drop a photo here, or Choose a photo

Nothing is uploaded. The photo stays on your device, and leaves when you close the tab.

Step 2 of 4 · The cutout

Frame your face in the oval.

Drag the photo to move it. Use the slider to zoom. Chin near the bottom of the oval, a little air above the hair.

Step 3 of 4 · The eyes

Show me where your eyes are.

Drag each ring onto the centre of a pupil. This is where your irises get swapped for the pair I drew.

Step 4 of 4 · Alive

There you are.

Grey like mine, floating like mine, and watching your cursor with the eyes I drew. Save it, make it a profile picture, send it to someone who will get it.

The download is a transparent PNG, eyes looking straight at you.
