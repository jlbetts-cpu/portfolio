Make your own floating head.

The same cutout as the head on my homepage. Your photo, your color, and a pair of living eyes. Everything happens in your browser, nothing gets uploaded.

Step 1 of 4 · The photo

Start with your face.

Face the camera in even light, eyes open. A selfie works great.

Drop a photo here, or Choose a photo

Your photo stays on your device. Close the tab and it is gone.

Step 2 of 4 · The cutout

Frame your face.

Drag to move. Slide to zoom. Chin near the bottom, a little room above your hair. The cut follows a head shape, tapered at the chin.

Step 3 of 4 · The eyes

Mark your face.

We take a first guess at where everything sits — just nudge what's off. Dots on the corners, top, and bottom of each eye; one dot each for brows, nose, and mouth. Drag a dot to fix it — or grab the middle of an eye to move the whole eye at once.

Drag near a marker to move it

Step 4 of 4 · Alive

There you are.

Floating, blinking, watching your cursor. It now lives on my homepage, hopping around next to my head. Go say hi. Save it, or send it to someone who will get it.
